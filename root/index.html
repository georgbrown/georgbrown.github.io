I'm a Computer Science Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Minnesota. My advisor is Rahul Narain, a professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi.

I received my M.S. degree in Physics at the University of Florida, and B.S. degrees in Physics and Astrophysics at the University of Minnesota.

My research interests span geometry optimization, physics-based animation, and numerical simulation. Recently I have worked on novel optimization-based methods for elastic deformation, mesh parameterization, and dynamic simulation with dissipative forces.
